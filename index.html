"Shut up before I take your batteries out" is a phrase I've heard for far too long. Pages and pages of android memes flood my Instagram feed, as well as my Twitter. Apple users would say things like "androids suck", "trash camera quality that", "the headphone jack is at the top of the phone" and much more. Personally, I am S I N C E R E L Y fed up with it. Apple may have an intuitive UI and a sleek, futuristic design, but that doesn't mean that it's a good phone. Take for example, Apple's iPhone XR, compared to the Samsung Galaxy S10e which are both $750 (at the time of me writing this, August 2019).
